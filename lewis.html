Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton, born on January 7, 1985, is one of the most successful drivers in Formula 1 history. He debuted in 2007 with McLaren, winning his first World Championship in 2008. In 2013, he joined Mercedes-AMG Petronas, where he dominated the turbo-hybrid era, securing six more titles between 2014 and 2020.
As of the 2025 season, Hamilton continues to race for Mercedes with his unmatched determination and racecraft. With 103 Grand Prix wins and over 190 podiums, he remains a major force on the grid and a global icon, both as a sportsman and activist pushing for diversity and sustainability in motorsport.
